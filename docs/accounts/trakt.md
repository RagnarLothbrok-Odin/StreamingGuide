# Trakt Account

Trakt tracks what you watch. We'll connect it to your app later.

## Sign Up

- Open: [Trakt sign up](https://trakt.tv/auth/signup_method)
- Choose your preferred sign-up method.

:::: tip Optional but Helpful
Automatic scrobbling keeps your watch history in sync across apps, note that your preferred will still automatically sync across your account with or without Trakt.
::::
